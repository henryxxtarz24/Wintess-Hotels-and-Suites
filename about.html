About Wintess Garden and Suites

Your trusted hospitality partner in Ifite, Awka since 2015

Our Story

A Legacy of Comfort & Hospitality

Founded in 2015, Wintess Garden and Suites began with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable accommodation for travelers visiting Awka. Located in the serene Ifite area, we've grown from a small guesthouse to a recognized 3-star hotel serving over 900 satisfied guests.

Our name reflects our commitment to being a "witness" to your memorable stays—whether you're a parent visiting your child at UNIZIK, a business traveler attending meetings in the capital, or a tourist exploring the rich culture of Anambra State.

We combine modern amenities with traditional Nigerian hospitality, ensuring every guest feels at home while enjoying the comfort and convenience they deserve.
